The purpose of this page is to present the different type of equipment in a Standard Classroom Setup. For information about how this equipment is wired, consult the Documentation page here.

DMPS-150
The DMPS-150 series is the most commonly used DMPS used across campus. It is commonly used in standard rooms.

DMPS-300
The DMPS-300 series is commonly used in non-standard rooms that require extra connections for the room to function.

Labgruppen Amplifier
The Labgruppen Amplifier is the most common used amplifier on campus and is used to control sound.

WIRES AND CABLES

Network Wires are used for devices to recieve IP addresses or power. They are also commonly referred to as "DM Lines" and "LAN cables" depending on the function of the cable.

Speaker Wires are commonly used to connect the amplifier to the DMPS. They allow the DMPS to control the sound in a room.

HDMI Cords are used to connect devices to each other and provide image in some cases. They also are able to transmit both audio and video and are one of the most common cables.

DisplayPort Cords are very similar to HDMIs. They share a common function, but are used in cases where HDMI ports are not available.
